    Tipsexpand_less
    • Weapon active slots: lh-1 and rh-1.
    • Click on the weapon parameters to view attack power, spell buff, absorptions, resistances and requirements.
    • Tooltips show more accurate values of absorption and resistance.
    • To use self and weapon buffs, you must select spell tool.
    • To show spell attack, you must select spell tool.
    • Buffed weapon must be selected in active slot. Spell tool must be selected in left(for magic weapon) or right (for magic shield) hand.
    • To show arrow attack and bolt attack, you must select bow and crossbow.
    • In the attribute input field press up or down keys on keyboard to increase or decrease the attribute value.
    • To use the "Dragon Head" or "Dragon Torso", use the appropriate stones in item list.
